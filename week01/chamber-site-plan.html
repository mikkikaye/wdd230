Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Mckenzie Hess

WDD 230

Overview

Purpose

My site will allow the members--individual businesses--to promote their interests, drive tourism, and attract more businesses to Lindon, Utah.

Site Name

The site name will be Lindon Chamber of Commerce which demonstrates which city it is and that it pertains to a chamber of commerce.

Scenarios

  • What events are being held in Lindon on a particular day or month?
  • Where can I find a list of Lindon businesses?
  • What initiatives are Lindon taking to promote business growth?
